Each built around hundreds of hours of real-world practice ownership — and the things I wish someone had handed me a decade ago.

GPS · Group Practice Studio

The Group Practice Course

For accidental entrepreneurs running real group practices.

A step-by-step, self-paced course covering everything from hiring and supervision to KPIs, culture, financial health, and sustainable growth. Designed for owners who've already built something real and need the systems to match.

Inside the course:

  • 8 modules · ~25 video lessons
  • Templates & SOPs you can copy/paste into your practice
  • Private community for course members
  • Optional live Q&A cohorts

Ships when it's done. I'd rather build it right than ship it on a schedule.

SPS · Solo Practice Studio

The Solo Practice Course

For solo therapists building a practice that fits their life.

Designed for solo practitioners who want a practice that works without becoming their whole identity. From niche clarity to fee setting, from scheduling to systems, all built around the assumption that you're not trying to scale to 40 clinicians.

Inside the course:

  • 6 modules · ~18 video lessons
  • Fee-setting worksheets & profit calculator
  • Private community for course members
  • Lifetime updates as the course evolves

No hustle culture. No "you should be seeing 30 clients a week." Just practical guidance.
