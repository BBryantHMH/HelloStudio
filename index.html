A little about me

Hi, I'm Bailey.

I'm a licensed psychologist, the founder of Hello Mental Health (a group practice I built from the ground up), and someone who has made — and learned from — every mistake a group practice owner can make.

I started Hello Studio because the business advice I needed when I was scaling didn't exist in a form that felt right for the kind of practice I was trying to build. Something heart-centered. Profitable. Sustainable. Led with intention.

That's what I help you build.
