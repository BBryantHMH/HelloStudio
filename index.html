Founder · Hello Studio

Founder & Business Coach

Dr. Bailey Bryant

Psychologist by training, accidental entrepreneur by necessity. I built a group practice from the ground up — hiring, scaling, leading a team, running the numbers, plus all the messy human moments that don't make it onto the LinkedIn version.

Over a decade of doing this, I've learned what actually works, what just looks good on Instagram, and exactly which mistakes you don't have to make. I started Hello Studio because the kind of business advice I needed when I was scaling didn't exist in a form that felt right for the kind of practice I was building — heart-centered, profitable, sustainable, led with intention.

That's what I help you build.

Studio Guide · Admin & Tech

Andrea Antczak (she/her)

Andrea is the kind of person who's done it all — and somehow made it all make sense together. With a background in substance abuse counseling, a reflexology practice she ran for years, and Reiki master training, she brings deep roots in the healing world to everything she does.

As Practice Coordinator at Hello Mental Health and a Studio Guide here, Andrea channels that same energy into helping mental health professionals build smarter, more sustainable practices — whether that means implementing the latest AI tools, demystifying SEO and AIO for practitioners who'd rather focus on their clients, or holding the kind of calm, grounded presence that makes figuring out your next move feel a lot less daunting.

Currently living and working from Albania — yes, really, she's a full-time digital nomad — Andrea is proof that you can build a meaningful, connected career from anywhere. She coaches practice coordinators, intake coordinators, and the admin humans who keep clinical teams running.
