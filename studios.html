What's a Studio?

Your go-to spot for whatever you actually need right now.

Not what some guru says you should need. Looking for a quick hit of clarity? Grab a workshop. Ready to go deep? Take a course. Want someone in your corner who actually gets it? Book 1:1 coaching.

Pick your Studio below and engage however feels right.

A Welcome from Bailey

Find the Studio that fits you.

A quick hello and a walk-through of the Studios — so you can pick the one that actually meets you where you are.

Group Practice

Group Practice Studio

For accidental entrepreneurs.

Group Practice Studio is for accidental entrepreneurs who need to get their operations sorted, their team actually functioning, and their sanity back — without turning into some soulless CEO or pretending you want to scale to 47 locations.

Learn how to lead like a human, build systems that don't make you want to scream, and create a practice that works for everyone (including you). Because you didn't build this thing just to be stressed out and exhausted all the time.

Solo Practice

Solo Practice Studio

Flying solo doesn't mean figuring everything out alone.

Whether you're just starting out and overwhelmed by the sheer volume of decisions, comfortably established but feeling stuck or stale, or somewhere in between wondering what your next move should be — Solo Practice Studio helps you build a practice that actually fits.

Get clear on your niche (for real this time), set fees that don't make you secretly resentful, create systems that run without you micromanaging every detail, and design a schedule that doesn't burn you out.

SPS Course · coming Workshops · coming 1:1 Coaching

Virtual Admin

Virtual Admin Studio

For the people who keep practices running.

The admin side of running a practice shouldn't require a PhD in figuring-everything-out… but it kind of does. Virtual Admin Studio is for practice coordinators, intake coordinators, VAs, practice managers, and anyone else handling the behind-the-scenes logistics that make or break a smooth operation.

Learn how to set up EHRs that don't make you cry, master scheduling systems that actually work, demystify SEO and AI tools, and build workflows that scale with the practices you support.

Grounded + Witchy

WooDio

Because sometimes the best leadership move is pulling a tarot card.

WooDio is for practice owners, clinical supervisors, team leaders, and administrators who know that running a therapy business requires more than logic and strategy — it also requires intuition, energy work, and trusting what you can't put in a spreadsheet.

Whether you open team meetings with meditation, use astrology to plan your quarterly goals, work with Human Design in supervision, or just honor your gut when something feels off — this Studio is for you. Permission granted to be both grounded and witchy.
